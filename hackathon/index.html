Frequently Ask Questions?

Who should apply?

Everyone! Developers, Students, Data Analyst, Data Enthusiasts, Designers, game programmers, makers, UI/UX designers, artists, animators, storytellers, enthusiasts, professionals, students, and all that want to spend time creating solutions with Data.

I Registered! Does This Guarantee Me A Spot In The Hack?

No. Due to limited space, we will be shortlisting participants based on the quality of their hackathon idea, the team composition and their demonstration of the ability to execute. If you are selected you will get an email with further processes.
Registration closes on September 9th at 11.59pm GMT.

What Type Of Things Should We Build?

The three themes for the hackathon are Climate, Spending, and Election. The idea and imagination are your, you can build anything which can be the best solution in these 3 sectors.

What Language Or Platform Can I Use?

Your choice, your confident and your experience but by the end of the day, we want your project to be more data-driven.

Who Owns The Code After We're Done?

Of course, You do! OKN does not one any of your code but we encourage you to open source it so that others can further expand on it.
